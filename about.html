Built for people learning to code.

For newcomers to code, the intricacies of hosting are pretty uninviting: buy hosting space, purchase domain name, link the two up - all this before the files get a look in. Happy Host's a free, easy-to-use tool that lets developers step around this process and get a site online in just a couple of steps.

Happy Host negotiates Git, the industry standard software, on your behalf. When you drop your files off, Teepee syncs with Github's servers, elects a spot for your code and feeds it in. Once it's settled, out comes your URL. Your website is live.

How it works.

Why Github?

It's the world's largest online storage space. It's free. It operates with next-to-no downtime. It doesn't interfere with your intellectual property rights (your files are still yours). And in joining Github, you plug yourself into the biggest development network in the world. The ability to interact with fellow developers, to learn from them, and collaborate with them, makes Github the perfect storm not just for hosting, but for improving your code.
